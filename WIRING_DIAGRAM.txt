DHT22 SENSOR:
  Pin 1 (Left)  → VCC (Power)
  Pin 2         → DATA (Signal)
  Pin 3         → Not used
  Pin 4 (Right) → GND (Ground)

LDR VOLTAGE DIVIDER:
  5V/3.3V ──┬── LDR ──┬── A0 (Analog Input)
            │         │
            │     10kΩ Resistor
            │         │
            └─────────┴── GND

LED CIRCUIT:
  D13/GPIO13 ──┬── 220Ω Resistor ──┬── LED Anode (+)
               │                   │
               │              LED Cathode (-)
               │                   │
               └───────────────────┴── GND
